About FRIDAY

FRIDAY is an advanced, intelligent Discord chatbot designed to bring a new level of interaction and utility to your server. With its natural language processing capabilities, FRIDAY can handle a wide range of tasks, from answering questions to managing server activities. It's more than just a bot—it's a helpful member of your community.

Features

  • **Intelligent Conversations:** Talk to FRIDAY just like you would to a person. It understands context and provides helpful, engaging responses.
  • **Server Moderation:** Keep your community safe with powerful moderation commands.
  • **Customizable:** Tailor FRIDAY's responses and features to fit your server's unique needs.

Commands

Command Description
`!help` Shows a list of all available commands and their usage.
`!ask ` Ask FRIDAY anything, and it will use its intelligence to provide a detailed answer.
`!ping` Checks the bot's latency to ensure it's online and responsive.
